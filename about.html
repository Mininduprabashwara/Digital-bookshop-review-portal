About Us

Bringing the world’s best books to readers across Sri Lanka—fast and reliably.



Do you believe that a book can change your life?

In 2015, Nishan De Silva—our Chief Bookaholic—picked up a book called The 4-Hour Work Week by Tim Ferriss.

That book led him to start a blog.                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                    That blog led him to start an online bookstore.

So, what do you think?                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                Can a book change your life?

We definitely think so.

That’s why our mission is simple: to bring some of the world’s best, life-changing books to readers across Sri Lanka.

Every title at Jumpbooks is carefully curated by a true Bookaholic—someone who would probably eat books if it meant learning something new. That’s how strongly we believe in the power of reading.

Happy reading!
